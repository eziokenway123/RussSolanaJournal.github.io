This week when I search the internet to look for things that can be used in this journal or to help me visualize one, something snapped and I realize that a good leader leads by example rather than just word itself. Let me phrase this so you can understand me, if a leader just told you what to do and doesn't help on the task on hand it can be pretty demoralizing and you would probably think the leader is lazy and doesn't know anything. True leadership, in contrast, involves not only guiding and directing but also actively demonstrating a commitment to the shared goals and tasks, inspiring and motivating the team by setting a compelling example.
